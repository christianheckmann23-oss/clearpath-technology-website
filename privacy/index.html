Privacy Policy

ClearPath Technology Partners LLC  |  Effective date: June 18, 2026  |  Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Who We Are

ClearPath Technology Partners LLC ("ClearPath," "we," "our," or "us") is a managed IT services provider based in Ohio. We provide Microsoft Cloud migrations, workflow automation, AI agents, website design and hosting, SEO, and managed security services to regional businesses. Our website is located at clearpathtechnology.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: directly from you when you fill out a form, and automatically when you visit our website.

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and job title
  • Business or company name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Industry and services you are interested in
  • Any additional details you include in a free-form message field

Information collected automatically:

  • Pages visited, time on page, and referring URL
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • IP address (anonymized where applicable)
  • Interactions with forms, buttons, and links (conversion events)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for legitimate business purposes related to the services you requested or expressed interest in:

  • To respond to your assessment or inquiry request within one business day
  • To prepare a written audit or migration plan tailored to your business
  • To follow up regarding services you checked or described
  • To send relevant information about ClearPath services you have indicated interest in
  • To improve our website content, page performance, and conversion experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only with service providers that help us operate our business, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Make.com - Our workflow automation platform processes form submissions and routes your inquiry to our team. Make stores the data in their EU-based infrastructure under their data processing agreement.
  • Microsoft Azure / Microsoft 365 - Our internal email, communication, and cloud infrastructure runs on Microsoft services. Inquiries delivered to our team may pass through Microsoft servers.
  • Vercel - Some pages of our website are hosted on Vercel's edge network. Vercel may log request metadata (IP address, headers) for security and performance purposes.
  • Google Analytics 4 - We use GA4 to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Google Business Profile - If you contact us through Google Search or Google Maps, your message is handled through Google's platform under their privacy policy.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of ClearPath, our clients, or the public.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. These include:

  • Essential cookies - Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies - Set by Google Analytics to track page views, session duration, and events. These are anonymous and aggregated.
  • Performance cookies - Used by Vercel and our hosting infrastructure to route traffic efficiently and monitor uptime.

You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and inquiry data for up to 24 months, or longer if an active client relationship exists. Analytics data is retained per the default retention settings of each platform (typically 14 months for GA4). You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted securely to Make.com via encrypted webhooks. Our internal systems are managed through Microsoft Entra ID with multi-factor authentication, conditional access policies, and Microsoft Defender endpoint protection - the same security stack we deploy for our clients.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use industry-standard protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are directed at businesses and business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our site, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you may contact us to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of future marketing communications at any time

We will respond to all verifiable requests within 30 days. To unsubscribe from emails, use the unsubscribe link in any message we send you or contact us directly.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites such as Microsoft product pages, Google documentation, or client websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated via a notice on our website. Continued use of our site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
