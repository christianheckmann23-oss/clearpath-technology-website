Why we don't lock you into one platform.

Most IT providers are resellers. Their recommendations are shaped by what they're certified in or what earns them the highest margins - not what's best for your business.

We're independent. That means we can tell you when Azure is the right call, when Vercel is cheaper and faster, when Make is the better automation tool, or when a combination of two platforms solves a problem neither could handle alone.

The result is a stack that's built around your outcomes - not around our vendor relationships.

How we choose tools

  • Cost efficiency We look at total cost including licensing, overhead, and time - not just the sticker price.
  • Integration fit The best tool is the one that connects to what you already use without adding friction.
  • Maintainability If we build it, you should be able to understand it. We avoid over-engineered solutions.
  • Scalability What works at 5 employees should still work at 50. We build for where you're going.
