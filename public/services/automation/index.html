Why Make and n8n instead of just Power Automate?

Power Automate is solid inside the Microsoft ecosystem. But most businesses have tools that don't live inside Microsoft - a CRM, a scheduling app, a payment processor, a Google workspace. Make and n8n connect to virtually anything via API or webhook, which means your automation isn't limited to what Microsoft supports.

We use the right tool for the job. Sometimes that's Make. Sometimes it's n8n for more complex, self-hosted pipelines. Sometimes both, working together. We're not locked into one platform - so you're not either.
