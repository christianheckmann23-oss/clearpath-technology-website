## Service Delivery Model

CirroFlow operates as a boutique consulting firm with a lean core team and specialized contractor network for project delivery:

### Primary Service Lines (public-facing, productized)

As of the July 2026 repositioning, CirroFlow's public brand and packages (Foundation/Growth/Accelerate) are built around six core services:

1. **Web Design** — Custom and templated site builds, conversion-focused
2. **Website Hosting** — Hosting, uptime monitoring, SSL, backups
3. **SEO** — Technical, on-page, and local SEO
4. **AI Search Optimization (GEO)** — Visibility in ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity
5. **AI Call Agents & Receptionists** — 24/7 lead capture via AI voice agents
6. **AI Automations** — Workflow automation, scoped via free consultation

Google Business Profile + Gmail/Workspace setup is bundled into packages as an onboarding item, not a standalone service line. Social Media Management and Advertising are metered add-ons, not core packages.

Point Contact for all lines above: Principal Founder.

### Cloud & IT Advisory (separate, custom-quoted lane)

**Microsoft Cloud & Security** (Azure migrations, Microsoft 365, Entra ID, Defender, Intune, Infrastructure as Code) has been spun off the public homepage and package ladder. It's now a standalone, off-nav, custom-quoted offering (site page: `/cloud-advisory`) for existing relationships and direct referrals — not part of the productized SMB packages. Point Contact: Principal Founder.

## Governance & Decision Making

- **Single Decision Point:** Principal Founder reviews and approves all client engagements and technical decisions
- **Quality Standards:** All deliverables (code, documentation, migrations) reviewed by Principal Founder before client delivery
- **Capacity:** Typically manages 4-8 active client relationships at any given time, with headroom for growth
- **E&O Insurance:** Professional liability coverage provides client protection
- **Certifications:** Microsoft Partner status, Azure certifications, industry compliance credentials

---

## Growth Strategy

As CirroFlow scales, the organization is structured to add:
1. **Senior Consultant** — Second principal-level technical resource
2. **Account Manager** — Client success and relationship specialist
3. **Delivery Engineers** — Full-time staff for Azure migrations and automation projects
4. **Operations** — Finance, billing, compliance, HR

Current model is sustainable for regional market penetration and allows rapid pivots based on client demand and market shifts.
