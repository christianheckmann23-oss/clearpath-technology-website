Use cases

  • The website is slow, hard to edit, or expensive to host.
  • Forms do not reliably reach the right person or system.
  • SEO pages need a better technical foundation and clearer service structure.
  • Hosting, DNS, SSL, and redirects are owned by too many vendors.
  • The business needs a modern site without adding WordPress maintenance overhead.

How it is implemented

  • Audit the current site, hosting, DNS, redirects, forms, analytics, and SEO risks.
  • Plan the page structure, calls to action, content, and migration path.
  • Build responsive pages with performance, accessibility, schema, and tracking in mind.
  • Deploy to Azure or Vercel with SSL, redirects, monitoring, and version control.
  • Connect forms to email, CRM, automations, and analytics reporting.
