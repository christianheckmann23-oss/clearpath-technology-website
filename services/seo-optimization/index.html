Use cases

  • The site has traffic but weak lead conversion from service pages.
  • Google Search Console shows indexing, crawl, or Core Web Vitals issues.
  • The business needs dedicated pages for high-value services and locations.
  • AI search tools are summarizing competitors instead of your business.
  • Local rankings depend on reviews, citations, and clearer service signals.

How it is implemented

  • Run a baseline audit with search, crawl, speed, and analytics data.
  • Prioritize fixes that affect indexation, rankings, conversion, or trust.
  • Rewrite titles, descriptions, headings, FAQs, internal links, and schema.
  • Improve performance and technical structure with the web team when needed.
  • Rerun the audit and track rankings, impressions, clicks, and leads.
