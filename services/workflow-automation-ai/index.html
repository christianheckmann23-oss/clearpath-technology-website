Automation + AI

Turn repeatable office work into managed workflows.

ClearPath builds workflow automations and AI agents that answer leads, move data between apps, read documents, trigger review requests, and keep teams from retyping the same information all day.

Automate My Workflow

Workflow Automations

Power Automate and Azure Logic Apps route form submissions, tasks, alerts, emails, approvals, and CRM updates.

AI Agents

AI assistants can qualify leads, summarize conversations, draft responses, search internal knowledge, and prepare next steps for staff.

Document Intelligence

Azure AI extracts structured data from PDFs, photos, invoices, handwritten forms, and field paperwork.

Use cases

  • New quote requests need SMS, email, CRM logging, and owner alerts within minutes.
  • Completed jobs should trigger review requests and reputation follow-up.
  • Field measurements, invoices, or order forms need to become clean data.
  • Teams need an AI assistant to answer common questions from approved sources.
  • Manual copy-and-paste work slows sales, operations, or admin staff.

How it is implemented

  • Map the workflow trigger, decision points, exceptions, and destination systems.
  • Build the flow in Power Automate, Azure Logic Apps, Azure Functions, or Microsoft Graph.
  • Connect approved data sources such as Microsoft 365, SharePoint, CRM, websites, and databases.
  • Add logging, failure alerts, permissions, and human approval steps where needed.
  • Test with real examples, document ownership, and tune the process after launch.

Implementation path

Step 01

Map

We document the manual process exactly as it works today, including edge cases.

Step 02

Prototype

A small working version proves the trigger, data movement, and notifications.

Step 03

Harden

Step 04

Operate

We monitor outcomes and adjust prompts, routing, or business rules as the team learns.
